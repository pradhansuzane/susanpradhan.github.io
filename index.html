Biography

Public Health Professional with six years experience in domains of Maternal and Child Health, Non-Communicable Diseases, and Health System Strengthening. My extensive experience within the public health sector has honed my expertise in critical areas such as project management, stakeholder engagement, data analysis, and comprehensive report writing.
I am deeply committed to my passion for enhancing the quality and availability of healthcare services for marginalized and vulnerable populations. I am committed to actively contributing to the achievement of global health goals and aspirations.
